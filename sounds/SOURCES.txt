Sound Attribution
=================

Crow distress / predator call audio downloaded from:
  HME Products — Free Predator Calls
  https://www.hmeproducts.com/sounds-download/

These files are redistributed solely for use with the crowbuster
project as a non-commercial, educational reference. All rights remain
with HME Products. If you are HME Products and want them removed,
please open an issue on the repository.

If you publish this repo, link back to the HME Products page
above rather than hosting the mp3s on the public repo.

Cat-deterrent audio in sounds/cat/:
  ultrasonic-20khz.mp3 — generated locally with sox:
    sox -n -r 48000 -c 1 ultrasonic-20khz.mp3 synth 8 sine 20000 vol 0.9
  See docs/cat-deterrence.md for the rationale (ultrasonic only, not
  audible distress audio — crow-style files actively attract cats).
